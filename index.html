What is OnBoard?

OnBoard is a grade and assignment manager, similar to Google Classroom and Skyward. OnBoard rises above the competition by showing scores in a quick and easy-to-understand layout.

How much does OnBoard cost?

What makes OnBoard better than the rest?

Although using features similar to Skyward and other grade management solutions, OnBoard expands on these by including stuff like our Instant Message System, which utilizes AI to filter messages and pictures; our homework pass, which allows teachers to set certain assignments able to use a homework pass (which is tailored to that teacher); or our grade calculator, which allows students to calculate how badly their grade would suffer if they were to get a bad score on something.
